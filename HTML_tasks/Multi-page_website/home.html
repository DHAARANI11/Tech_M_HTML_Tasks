About us

Rapido is a ride-hailing service that offers bike taxis, auto rickshaws, and taxicabs in India. It also provides parcel delivery and logistics services. Rapido is based in Bengaluru and was founded in 2015.
Features
Safety: Rapido offers insured rides, and provides helmets for bike rides.
Payment: Rapido offers cashless payment options such as PayTM, UPI, and Rapido Wallet.
Tracking: Rapido offers live tracking of rides.
App: The Rapido app is available on Android and iOS platforms.
Languages: The Rapido app is available in English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada.
