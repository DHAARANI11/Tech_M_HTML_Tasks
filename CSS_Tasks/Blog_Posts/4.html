Blogs

Tech Crunch

The TechCrunch website’s clean layout prioritizes text readability with its simple white background and black text. Headlines of the most recent articles are shown neatly on the homepage and accompanied by snippets from the blog posts and relevant pictures to add a splash of color. The TechCrunch website also provides a helpful sidebar on the left side of the screen for easy navigation.

The Verge

The Verge’s website homepage is vibrant – a black and white theme with bright accents of orange and magenta. There is a collage of large headlines and pictures to attract site visitors’ attention below the blog’s title and the navigation bar. In addition, some additional blog content is displayed on the right side of the homepage for easy access, such as featured videos and reviews.
